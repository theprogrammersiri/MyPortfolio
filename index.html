01 — About

A builder who never stopped being curious

It started with rugs. Seriously. My first professional role was designing intricate Tibetan patterns for international clients at Exclusive Rug Graphics — teaching me that every pixel, every line, every detail matters. That design instinct never left.

Then came code. Pursuing a BScIT at Presidential Graduate School (affiliated with Westcliff University, California), I discovered that building for the web was the closest thing to pure creative freedom I'd ever found. From crafting responsive UIs to exploring cloud infrastructure on AWS, I've spent every semester pushing what I can build.

Today I'm a final-year student sitting at the intersection of design and development — someone who cares just as much about how something looks as how it works. I speak three languages, hold AWS certifications, and believe the best digital experiences come from people who think in systems but feel in stories.
